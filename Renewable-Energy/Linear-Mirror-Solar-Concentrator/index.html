Linear Mirror Solar Concentrator

High-Efficiency Solar Thermal System for Sustainable Energy

Overview

The Linear Mirror Solar Concentrator is a high-efficiency solar thermal system designed to deliver concentrated heat for agricultural and industrial applications. It uses flat mirrors with a precision tracking system to focus sunlight onto a fixed heat exchanger, achieving temperatures of up to 200 Â°C.

Technical Features

  • Mirror Array: 32 flat mirrors arranged in eight modular sections.
  • Tracking System: Motorized precision tracking for optimal solar alignment.
  • Robust Construction: Galvanized steel and aluminum frame, wind-resistant up to 200 km/h.
  • Energy Efficient: Extremely low power consumption (~2 W) for tracking motors.
  • Thermal Integration: Compatible with solar-air and solar-water heat exchangers.

Applications

The Linear Mirror Solar Concentrator is suitable for a wide range of renewable-energy-driven applications, including greenhouse heating, biomass drying, district heating support, and industrial thermal processes. When combined with biomass gasification, it enables continuous and dispatchable clean energy generation.
