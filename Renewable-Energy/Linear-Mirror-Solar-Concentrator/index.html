Linear Mirror Solar Concentrator

Overview

The Linear Mirror Solar Concentrator is a high-efficiency solar thermal system designed to deliver concentrated heat for agricultural and industrial applications. It uses flat mirrors with a precision tracking system to focus sunlight onto a fixed heat exchanger, achieving high-temperature output up to 200Â°C.

Key Features

  • 32 flat mirrors arranged in eight sections with motorized tracking
  • Robust construction: galvanized steel and aluminum, wind-resistant up to 200 km/h
  • Efficient solar-thermal conversion for greenhouse heating and biomass processing
  • Low power consumption for motors (~2 W)
  • Integration with solar-air or solar-water heat exchangers

Applications

Suitable for solar heating, biomass drying, greenhouse energy, and industrial thermal processes, providing a sustainable and renewable energy solution.
