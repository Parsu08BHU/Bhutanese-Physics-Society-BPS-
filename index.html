About the Bhutanese Physical Society

The Bhutanese Physical Society is dedicated to promoting physics education, research, and collaboration in Bhutan. We engage students, schools, and researchers through outreach programs, workshops, and conferences to foster curiosity, learning, and a strong scientific community.

The Society plans to organize conferences, workshops, seminars, and seasonal schools in physics and related fields, bringing together local and international experts and exposing participants to current advances in physics research and education.

Through conferences, outreach, collaborative projects, and open platforms such as GitHub, the Society aspires to cultivate a sustainable and vibrant physics community in Bhutan.

Vision

The Society envisions a Bhutan where physics serves people, the environment, and future generations, aligned with Gross National Happiness.

Mission

To promote physics education, research, and responsible applications for sustainable national development.

Objectives

  1. Strengthen physics education
  2. Encourage renewable energy research
  3. Nurture young physicists
  4. Promote ethical applications of physics
