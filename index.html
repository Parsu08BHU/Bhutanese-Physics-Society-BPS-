About the Bhutanese Physical Society

The Bhutanese Physical Society is dedicated to promoting physics education, research, and collaboration in Bhutan. The Society engages students, schools, and researchers through outreach programs, workshops, seminars, and conferences to foster curiosity, learning, and a strong scientific community.

Vision

The Bhutanese Physical Society envisions a Bhutan where the understanding of nature through physics serves the well-being of people, environmental protection, and future generations.

Mission

To promote physics education, research, and responsible application of physics for sustainable development and national well-being.

Objectives

  1. Promote quality physics education
  2. Encourage renewable energy and environmental research
  3. Nurture young physicists and researchers
  4. Support ethical applications of physics
